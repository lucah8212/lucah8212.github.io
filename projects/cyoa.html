CYOA Project

Context

For this new project, we were once again given directions by Mr. Mueller. The goal was to create your own story through GitHub by intertwining different parts of the story through links. One thing that was really helpful in understanding this was the demo we were given at the very beginning which involved the story of either waking up or ignoring your alarm which would lead to certain events such as being fired if you didn't wake up or getting a virus from using your computer at work. We were told to brainstorm any topic in which we would choose our 5 favorite, and then we would be matched with a partner. Me and Valentino chose a story about being a driver in a Monaco Grand Prix.

Content

Right off the bat, we were told to create a web tree of what our story was going to be about and how one thing would lead to another. We did this on Google Drawings using shapes and arrows. Based on that, we would now create our own GitHub file tree with all these things that would lead to another thing.
